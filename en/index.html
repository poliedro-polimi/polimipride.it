About us PoliEdro is a student's association from Politecnico di Milano involved since 2012 in raising awareness about socio-cultural issues related to the LGBTI+ community. Created by LGBTI+ students but addressed to everyone, PoliEdro aims at being something to rely on for anyone looking for a debate on topic linked to the community, and does so through informative meetings, interviews, film viewings and debates
