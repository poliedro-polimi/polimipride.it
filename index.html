LGBTI+ students of PoliMi

We are a group of students of Politecnico di Milano. We want to raise awareness of the issues of the LGBTI+ community (Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Trans*, Intersexual and all the other nuances) by organizing events, conferences, talks and meetings around these topics. We try to be a landmark for students who feel discriminated in the University environment.
